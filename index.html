Welcome to The Library the goal of the Library Resource Center is to provide users excellent service and to meet their highest expectations for the successful pursuit of academic endeavors. The library will serve as a conduit to discovery and self-education. Meeting the information needs of its students, faculty, and staff by providing resources, to support curriculum objectives, and enabling users to become information literate. While acquiring and organizing information resources, acting as a guide to information utilizing traditional formats and innovative technology.

The staff is fully committed to helping users access the most up-to-date global information available to support and develop their skills, and preparing them for lifelong learning.
